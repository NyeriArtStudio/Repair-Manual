Honda HR-V Wiring Diagrams

Comprehensive wiring diagrams extracted from the Honda HR-V factory shop manual. All Honda wire colour codes shown. Covers all model codes (KG, KE, KY, KT, KQ, KU, KN).

Honda Wire Colour Code

BLK = Black  |  RED = Power (+B, IG)  |  WHT = Signal/Data  |  GRN = Sensor ground  |  BLU = Control signal  |  YEL = Sensor signal  |  BRN = Ground (GND)  |  ORG = Illumination  |  GRY = Tachometer  |  PNK = Fuel injection  |  PUR = ABS/VSA

Available Diagrams

ECM pinout, fuel injectors, ignition coil, sensors (MAP, TPS, ECT, IAT, O2), fuel pump relay, VTEC solenoid (D16W5), and knock sensor.

Charging & Starting

Battery, ignition switch, starter, alternator, charging circuit, PGM-FI main relay, and multi-fuse box.

ABS System

ABS modulator, wheel speed sensors, ABS ECU, ABS pump motor, and ABS indicator circuit.

CVT / A/T Control

CVT ECU, shift solenoids, gear position switch, mode indicator, and 4WD control unit (where fitted).

Lighting System

Headlights, position lights, tail lights, brake lights, reverse lights, turn signals, hazard warning, and combination switch.

Body Electrical

Power windows, door locks, rear defogger, immobiliser, SRS, instrument cluster, and integrated control unit.

Cooling Fans & HVAC

Fan motors, fan relays, thermo switch, AC compressor clutch, blower motor, and power transistor.

Gauges & Instruments

Speedometer, tachometer, fuel gauge, temp gauge, warning lights, and instrument illumination.

SRS Wiring Warning

All SRS-related wiring is in yellow harnesses. Never probe these with a multimeter on resistance mode - this can deploy airbags. Use only Honda PGM tester or dedicated SRS diagnostic equipment.
