Transmission

Front-wheel drive as standard with optional Real Time 4WD. Manual or CVT (Honda Multi Matic) available. Engine mounted transversely, transmission on left side.

Clutch

SpecificationValue
TypeSingle dry plate, diaphragm spring
FluidDOT 3 or DOT 4 (hydraulic operation)
Pedal free play5-15 mm (manual adjustment at master cylinder)
Master cylinderShared reservoir with brake fluid

Hydraulic clutch: Self-adjusting for wear but pedal height is adjustable at master cylinder pushrod. Do not confuse with cable type. If pedal feels spongy: bleed clutch system (bleeder on slave cylinder, 8mm wrench).

5-Speed Manual

SpecificationValue

Oil typeHonda MTF or API GL-4 10W-30
Change interval40,000 km
Reverse gearLift ring on selector or separate gate

Gearbox Oil Change

1

Drive to warm oil. Park level. Remove drain plug (17mm) and filler plug (17mm, upper).

2

Drain completely. Clean magnetic drain plug.

3

Refit drain plug: 35 Nm. Fill with correct oil until level with filler hole.

4

Refit filler plug: 35 Nm.

Use GL-4 Only

Do NOT use GL-5 gear oil in the Honda manual transmission. GL-5's extreme pressure additives attack the synchroniser brass rings, causing baulky gear changes and premature failure. Only use Honda MTF or API GL-4 10W-30.

CVT Fluid Warning

CVT fluid is specific and cannot be substituted with automatic transmission fluid. Using wrong fluid causes belt slip, jerking, and catastrophic CVT failure. Only use Honda CVT-F (green fluid). Check level with engine warm, idling, on level ground. Dipstick has upper/lower marks.

CVT Common Faults

SymptomCauseAction
Juddering at low speedWorn launch clutch, contaminated fluidChange CVT fluid first. If persists, internal repair needed.
High revs, poor accelerationBelt slip (worn belt or low pressure)Check fluid level/condition. May need CVT overhaul.
Noise (whining)Bearings worn, low fluidCheck fluid immediately. If full: internal damage likely.
Slipping between ratiosValve body fault, solenoid issueHonda diagnostic scan for codes.

Real Time 4WD System

Normally front-wheel drive. Rear wheels engaged via hydraulic multi-plate clutch when front wheel slip detected. No driver input required.

ComponentLocation
Rear differentialMounted on rear torsion beam
4WD clutchAdjacent to rear differential
PropshaftUnder vehicle, front to rear
4WD control unitUnder rear seat or boot area

Fluid: Rear differential uses Honda DPSF (Dual Pump Fluid) or 80W-90 hypoid gear oil. 0.9L capacity. Change every 40,000 km.

4WD warning light: If 4WD light illuminates: check rear differential fluid level, check 4WD control unit fuse, scan for DTCs. Common fault: 4WD clutch solenoid or speed sensor.

Source: Honda HR-V Shop Manual, Chapters 12-16.
