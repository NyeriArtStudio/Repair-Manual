VW Golf MK2 Wiring Diagrams

Interactive wiring diagrams extracted from the Haynes workshop manual. All component codes expanded to full English names. Wire colours shown as per factory colour codes. Includes relay upgrade recommendations for high-current circuits.

Wire Colour Code

Brown = Earth/Ground  |  Red = Switched +12V (ignition)  |  Black = Permanent +12V (battery)  |  Yellow = Starter circuit  |  Blue = Headlight circuit  |  Green = Instrument panel  |  White = Reverse/dimmer  |  Grey = Interior lights  |  Purple = Ignition/starter signal

Common Wiring Faults

SymptomCommon CauseCheck
Intermittent lights/instrumentsEarth point corrosionClean earth points under dash and behind headlights
Headlights dimWeak earth, old wiringVoltage at lamp vs battery. Add relay upgrade.
Heated screen weakHigh resistance in old wiringAdd relay with new heavy-gauge feed
Starter clicks, no crankPoor battery earth or main cableVoltage drop test on earth and live cables
Fan not workingBlown fuse, faulty switch, seized fanCheck fuse, jumper fan direct to battery
Dash lights flickerAlternator brushes worn, regulator failingCheck alternator output voltage

Source: Haynes Manual 1081, Chapter 12 (Wiring Diagrams). *Relay upgrade recommendations from automotive electrical best practices
